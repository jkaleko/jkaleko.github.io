About

Jason is an award-winning screenwriter and on-air promo writer with eight years experience in the film/tv industry. He is represented by United Talent Agency and Energy Entertainment, and is a current member of the Writers Guild of America, West.

As an on-air promo copywriter, Jason has written spots for companies such as FOX Sports, Adobe, Stun Creative, and Hawthorne Direct, and has worked on properties that include 24: Live Another Day, NASCAR, and Super Bowl XLVIII.

For his feature & television work, Jason has been recognized by awarding bodies such as Script Pipeline, TrackingB, the Nicholl Fellowship, and the Page Awards, amongst others.

He has a B.A. in Creative Writing from Carnegie Mellon University and is an avid New York Jets fan.

He is located in Los Angeles, CA.
